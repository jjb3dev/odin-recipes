Overnight Oats

Description

I love this overnight oats recipe! It's easy to make-ahead for a quick, on-the-go breakfast. You can add-in your favorite toppings, fruit, or nuts.

Looking for a make-ahead breakfast thatâ€™ll keep you full until lunch? Try overnight oats with yogurt!

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup rolled oats
  • 2 tsp Honey
  • 2 tsp chia seeds
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients together
  2. Mix thoroughly
  3. Cover and put into fridge overnight
  4. Enjoy the next day!
